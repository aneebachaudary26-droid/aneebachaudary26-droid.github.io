About

I develop PFAS‑free omniphobic textile coatings by synthesizing and stratifying Janus nanoparticles with cross‑linkable binders. My broader interests include surface engineering, sustainable materials, and supramolecular chemistry.

The goal is to deliver fluorine‑free performance—durability, repellency, and self‑cleaning—while reducing environmental impact.
