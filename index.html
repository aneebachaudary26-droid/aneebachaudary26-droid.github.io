About Me

I am developing PFAS-free omniphobic textile coatings by synthesizing and stratifying Janus nanoparticles with crosslinkable binders. My broader interests include surface engineering, sustainable materials, and supramolecular chemistry.

My research focuses on creating environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional fluorinated chemicals, contributing to the development of sustainable coating technologies that maintain high performance while reducing environmental impact.
